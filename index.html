Installation Instructions

For linux, you will need libsdl2 and libsdl2-mixer installed.

How to Play

Match up the colored candies to rot any adjacent teeth. You win when the patient's teeth are all gone. The arrow keys or A/D move left and right, S or down will drop the current piece faster, Q,W,E, and Up will rotate the piece. Enter or space to pause.
